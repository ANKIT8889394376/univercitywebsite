Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Computer Science is a rapidly growing field. Within the past 20 years conventional system and processing data has been replaced by computers. Innovation in Digital concept, e-education, virtual and robotics are new inventions. Mobile and video communication practice are household reality. Out of school as a Computer Science major, students can instantly find work in computer data and word processing sectors. E-Business, IT sectors, e-marketing, e-governance, on-line financial transactions, data inventory, computer aided analysis, designing, manufacturing, testing, automobile industries, railways, aero-space, satellite communication, remote sensing, transport sectors, need programmers and hardware engineers to address the issues. As a computer engineering major, one can work for top tech companies such as TCS, Apple, Google, Cisco, Intel, IBM, Windows, Samsung, etc.
